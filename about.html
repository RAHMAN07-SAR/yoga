About Us

Discover our story, mission, and the passionate instructors behind Bengaluru’s most trusted Yoga Studio.

Our Story

Founded in Bengaluru, Yoga Studio was created with a vision to make the ancient practice of yoga accessible to modern lives. With over 100,000+ students worldwide, we’re proud to blend tradition with innovation.

Our certified instructors specialize in different yoga styles including Hatha, Vinyasa, Yin, and Power Yoga, ensuring that each student finds a practice that fits their journey.
